Social Data 2022 - Motor Vehicle Collisions/Crashes

Introduction

Disadvantages of car collisions- disrupt traffic flow, cost resources, wastes fuel ,loss of life. Other than homicides, the fatal incidents with which police have the most contact with the public are fatal traffic collisions. So there is huge potential in building a traffic safety model that can help detect reasons of collisions early and rectifies them before unfortunate events.

We want to addresse the following SDG 3 "Good Health and well being" Target 3.6 Death Rate due to Road Traffic Injuries



This website aim at empowering you to investigate the Motor Vehicle Collisions in the US for instance when, where and the factors contributing to the accedent in order to gain more edcuation and knowladege on minmizing the the number of accedents and eventually the number of died people.

The goal of analyzing the data of Motor Vehicle Collisions

Given this interactive website, we want to cast light on several questions about the nature of Motor Vehicle Collisions in NewYork:

  • When does the most accedents happen
  • Where does the most accedents happen
  • When the next accedent happen
  • Where the next accendet happen
  • How many would die because of the accedent
